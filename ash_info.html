How to Play Ash

Role: Entry Fragger

Entry Fraggers are the first people to enter the building. This role assignment means that you will be trying to clear out roamers and gain map control for the rest of your team. Hunting these roamers allows your team to attack the object site much easier without having to worry about people flanking you. The same goes for map control, allowing your team to have map control means that you can easily start you execute on site allowing you to either plant the “bomb” or kill the rest of the enemy team. As the teams fragger you are meant to be taking gun fights. You need to be decisive and not afraid to take on a challenge.

Why Play Ash?

  • Versatile weapons
  • Very Fast (3 - speed)
  • Loadout compliments aggressive playstyle
  • Able to breach all destructible surfaces from range

What's Needed to play Ash

  • Excellent Map knowledge
  • Good mechanical skill
  • Fast reflexes
